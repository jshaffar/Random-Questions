The following is a list of notable deaths in December 1997.

Entries for each day are listed alphabetically by surname. A typical entry lists information in the following sequence:

  • Name, age, country of citizenship at birth, subsequent country of citizenship (if applicable), reason for notability, cause of death (if known), and reference.

December 1997[edit]

1[edit]

  • Julius Barnathan, 70, American broadcast engineer.[1]
  • Gerald Danovitch, 65, Canadian classical saxophone player and educator.
  • Stéphane Grappelli, 89, French-Italian jazz violinist.[2]
  • Władysław Młynek, 67, Polish teacher, writer and poet.
  • Khan Ataur Rahman, 68, Bangladeshi film actor and director and singer.
